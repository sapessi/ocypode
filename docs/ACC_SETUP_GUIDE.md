**Sim Racing Setup Decision Tree**

### **For Assetto Corsa Competizione**

Use this guide to identify where you are losing time or feeling discomfort in the car, and apply the corresponding changes.

## ---

**1\. Cornering Issues**

*Identify which part of the corner is causing the issue.*

### **üìç Corner Entry (Braking & Turn-in)**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: UNDERSTEER** (Car won't turn in / Pushes wide)  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Reduce Front Ride Height / Increase Rear Ride Height (Increase Rake).  
  * **Suspension:** Soften Front Springs / Stiffen Rear Springs.  
  * **Anti-Roll Bar:** Soften Front ARB.  
  * **Dampers:** Soften Front Bump / Stiffen Rear Rebound.  
  * **Brakes:** Move Brake Bias Rearward.  
  * **Alignment:** Increase Front Toe Out.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: OVERSTEER** (Rear slides out on entry)  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Increase Front Ride Height / Reduce Rear Ride Height (Reduce Rake).  
  * **Suspension:** Stiffen Front Springs / Soften Rear Springs.  
  * **Anti-Roll Bar:** Stiffen Front ARB.  
  * **Dampers:** Stiffen Front Bump / Soften Rear Rebound.  
  * **Brakes:** Move Brake Bias Forward.  
  * **Electronics:** Increase Diff Preload (Locks diff on coast).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: INSTABILITY** (Nervous / Twitchy)  
  * **Brakes:** Move Brake Bias Forward.  
  * **Suspension:** Stiffen Front Springs.  
  * **Drivetrain:** Increase Differential Preload.

### **üìç Mid-Corner (Coasting / Apex)**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: UNDERSTEER** (Car won't hold the line)  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Increase Front Wing / Splitter.  
  * **Anti-Roll Bar:** Soften Front ARB / Stiffen Rear ARB.  
  * **Suspension:** Soften Front Springs / Stiffen Rear Springs.  
  * **Geometry:** Increase Negative Front Camber.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: OVERSTEER** (Rear loses grip mid-turn)  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Increase Rear Wing.  
  * **Anti-Roll Bar:** Stiffen Front ARB / Soften Rear ARB.  
  * **Suspension:** Stiffen Front Springs / Soften Rear Springs.  
  * **Geometry:** Increase Negative Rear Camber.

### **üìç Corner Exit (Acceleration)**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: UNDERSTEER** (Can't get on power / Pushes wide on exit)  
  * **Drivetrain:** Increase Differential Preload / Locking.  
  * **Suspension:** Soften Front Springs / Stiffen Rear Springs.  
  * **Dampers:** Stiffen Rear Slow Bump / Soften Front Slow Rebound.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: OVERSTEER** (Power Oversteer / Spin on exit)  
  * **Drivetrain:** Reduce Differential Preload / Locking.  
  * **Suspension:** Stiffen Front Springs / Soften Rear Springs.  
  * **Electronics:** Increase Traction Control (TC1/TC2).  
  * **Dampers:** Soften Rear Slow Bump / Stiffen Front Slow Rebound.  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Increase Rear Wing (High speed exits).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: SUDDEN SNAP OVERSTEER**  
  * **Suspension:** Soften Rear Springs / Soften Rear ARB.  
  * **Dampers:** Soften Rear Fast Bump.

## ---

**2\. Straight Line & Braking**

### **üèÅ Straights**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: LOW TOP SPEED**  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Reduce Rear Wing Angle.  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Reduce Ride Height (Overall drag reduction).  
  * **Alignment:** Reduce Toe angles (closer to 0).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: BOTTOMING OUT** (Scraping sound / Speed loss)  
  * **Suspension:** Increase Ride Height.  
  * **Suspension:** Stiffen Springs (Prevent compression).  
  * **Dampers:** Stiffen Fast Bump.

### **üõë Braking**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: FRONT LOCKING**  
  * **Brakes:** Move Bias Rearward.  
  * **Brakes:** Reduce Brake Pressure/Power.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: REAR LOCKING**  
  * **Brakes:** Move Bias Forward.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: INSTABILITY UNDER BRAKING** (Wandering rear end)  
  * **Aerodynamics:** Reduce Rear Ride Height.  
  * **Suspension:** Stiffen Front Springs (Reduce dive).  
  * **Dampers:** Stiffen Front Bump.

## ---

**3\. Tyres & Geometry**

*Check tyre HUD or Telemetry after a few laps.*

### **üå°Ô∏è Temperature & Pressure**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è Tyres are OVERHEATING**  
  * **Ducts:** Open Brake Ducts (Cooling).  
  * **Setup:** Soften Suspension (Less energy put into tyres).  
  * **Driving:** Reduce aggression / steering angle.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è Tyres are COLD / GRAINING**  
  * **Ducts:** Close Brake Ducts (Retain heat).  
  * **Setup:** Stiffen Suspension (More energy put into tyres).  
  * **Alignment:** Increase Toe (Generates friction heat).

### **üìê Wear Patterns (Camber/PSI)**

* **‚ö†Ô∏è Inner Wear \> Outer Wear:** Too much Negative Camber.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è Outer Wear \> Inner Wear:** Too much Positive Camber (Not enough negative).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è Center Wear \> Edge Wear:** Pressure too High.  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è Edge Wear \> Center Wear:** Pressure too Low.

## ---

**4\. Race Conditions (Wet / End of Race)**

### **üåßÔ∏è Wet Track Setup**

* **Ride Height:** Increase (Avoid aquaplaning).  
* **Wings:** Increase Maximum Downforce.  
* **Suspension:** Soften Springs and ARBs significantly.  
* **Brakes:** Move Bias Rearward (Front grip is limited).  
* **Diff:** Lower Preload (Smoother rotation).

### **‚õΩ Fuel Load Changes**

* **Qualifying (Low Fuel):** Car is lighter. You can run lower ride heights and softer springs compared to full race load.  
* **Race (High Fuel):** Car is heavier. Increase ride height or stiffen springs to prevent bottoming out.

### ---

**üìù Key Legend**

* **Springs:** Controls weight transfer speed and pitch/roll magnitude.  
* **Dampers (Bump/Rebound):** Controls *how fast* the springs compress or extend.  
* **ARB (Anti-Roll Bar):** Connects left and right suspension; controls body roll balance.  
* **Aero (Wing/Splitter):** Air pressure pushing the car down for grip at speed.

## ---

**5\. Advanced Damping: Slow vs. Fast**

*Distinguish between **Driver Inputs** (Slow) and **Track Surface** (Fast) to fine-tune handling.*

**The Golden Rule:**

* **Slow Damping:** Controls the **Car's Body** (Pitch, Roll, Squat). Occurs during braking, steering, and accelerating.  
* **Fast Damping:** Controls the **Wheels** (Impacts). Occurs when hitting kerbs, bumps, or potholes.

### **üìç Kerbs & Bumps (Fast Damping)**

*This controls how the suspension reacts to sharp impacts (High shaft velocity).*

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: BOUNCING / LAUNCHING** (Car skips or jumps off the kerb)  
  * **Diagnosis:** The suspension is too stiff to absorb the impact energy.  
  * **Adjustment:** **Soften Fast Bump** (Allows wheel to retract up instantly).  
  * **Adjustment:** **Soften Fast Rebound** (Allows wheel to extend back down to track instantly).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: BOTTOMING OUT** (Hard metallic strike *after* a large bump)  
  * **Diagnosis:** The suspension compressed too easily and hit the bump stops.  
  * **Adjustment:** **Stiffen Fast Bump** (Slows down the compression on impact).  
  * **Adjustment:** **Increase Ride Height** (If damping alone cannot fix it).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: OSCILLATION AFTER BUMP** (Car continues to bounce after the kerb)  
  * **Diagnosis:** The spring energy isn't being controlled after the impact.  
  * **Adjustment:** **Stiffen Fast Rebound** (Stops the spring from extending too fast after compression).

### **üìç Driver Inputs & Chassis Balance (Slow Damping)**

*This controls how fast the car settles into a corner (Low shaft velocity).*

* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: LAZY / SLUGGISH RESPONSE** (Car feels like a boat)  
  * **Diagnosis:** Weight transfer is happening too slowly.  
  * **Adjustment:** **Stiffen Slow Bump** (Resists the roll/dive, making it feel sharper).  
  * **Adjustment:** **Stiffen Slow Rebound** (Controls the unloaded side from lifting too fast).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: HARSH / TWITCHY** (Car reacts too violently to inputs)  
  * **Diagnosis:** Weight transfer is too sudden, breaking traction.  
  * **Adjustment:** **Soften Slow Bump** (Slows down the roll, giving the tyre time to grip).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: EXCESSIVE DIVE (Braking)**  
  * **Action:** **Stiffen Front Slow Bump** (Support the nose).  
* **‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem: EXCESSIVE SQUAT (Acceleration)**  
  * **Action:** **Stiffen Rear Slow Bump** (Support the rear).

**üí° Cheat Sheet: Damping Grid**

| Issue | Relevant Damper Setting |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Car feels lazy turning in** | Stiffen Front **Slow** Bump |
| **Car oversteers on entry** | Stiffen Front **Slow** Bump / Stiffen Front **Slow** Rebound |
| **Car bounces off kerbs** | Soften **Fast** Bump / Soften **Fast** Rebound |
| **Car scrapes on bumps** | Stiffen **Fast** Bump |
| **Rear unstable exiting corners** | Soften Rear **Slow** Bump |
